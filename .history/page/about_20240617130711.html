    ABOUT KINTARO RAMEN

    KINTARO RAMEN, the pioneer of Tori-Paitan (Creamy Chicken broth) opened its first door in 2013 in Vancouver, Canada. Now many branches in region.

    Chicken is one of the universal foods that you can find in almost every culture, every country. This is how our signature Tori-Paitan (Creamy Chicken broth) was inspired and created, and being loved by many people from different background and cultures.

    We wish to continue to spread KINTARO RAMEN more and more.
